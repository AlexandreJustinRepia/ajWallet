# 🚀 Release Notes - v0.3.0 "The Management & Calculation" Update

Welcome to RootEXP v0.3.0! This "Mega Update" focuses on giving you total control over your financial data while making data entry faster and smarter than ever before.

---

## 🛒 Smart Shopping Evolution
The shopping experience has been completely overhauled from a simple list to a professional procurement tool.
- **Multi-List Management**: Create separate lists for Groceries, Tech, Travel, or specific projects.
- **Drafting & Autosave**: Never lose your progress. Start a list and save it as a draft for later.
- **Bulk Item Entry**: A dedicated bulk-add screen allows you to list everything at once before heading to the store.
- **Settlement Guard**: Smart logic prevents you from settling a list if your wallet balance is too low, keeping your books accurate.

## 🏷️ Custom Category Engine
You asked, we listened! You are no longer restricted to default categories.
- **Full CRUD Support**: Add, Edit, and Delete categories to match your lifestyle.
- **Icon & Color Customization**: Pick from a professional library of icons and themes for each category.
- **Manual Sorting**: Drag-and-drop your categories to prioritize what you use most.
- **Auto-Categorization**: Improved logic for detecting transaction types and suggesting labels based on your spending history.

## 🤝 Proactive Debts & Loans
Managing money between friends and family is now more robust.
- **Improved UI**: A cleaner, more informative debt overview with progress bars and status badges.
- **Repayment Tracking**: Dedicated flows for "Received Payment" and "Debt Payment" ensures you don't confuse initial loans with repayments.
- **Overpayment Safeguard**: The app now warns you if you're trying to pay back more than what is owed.

## 🧮 Integrated "Pro-Calc" Engine
The biggest productivity boost yet! We've removed the need to switch between your calculator app and RootEXP.
- **Calculator Keyboards**: Every amount input field in the app—from Split Bills and Squad Settlements to Shopping Prices—now features an integrated mathematical calculator.
- **On-the-fly Arithmetic**: Sum up multiple receipts or calculate unit prices directly within the input field.
- **Precise Settlements**: Ensure every cent is accounted for in squad group expenses and settlements.

## 🧠 AI Financial Insights
- **Category-Based Analysis**: The AI now groups your spending into "Essentials", "Leisure", and "Debt" to give you a higher-level view of your financial health.
- **Dynamic Advice**: Color-coded insights on your dashboard warn you of risky debt-to-income ratios or sudden spikes in discretionary spending.

---

### 🛠️ Improvements & Bug Fixes
- **Backup Stability**: Added non-dismissible loading indicators to prevent database corruption during import/export.
- **UI Polishing**: Enhanced glassmorphism effects and smoother transitions across all screens.
- **0 Issues Policy**: The entire codebase has been audited and cleared of all linting warnings and errors.
